Our Story

About Super Sensors

We believe the answers to some of medicine's hardest questions are in the air we breathe.

Mission

Our Mission

Super Sensors exists to democratize breath-based data collection. We build the tools that researchers, clinicians, and developers need to unlock the diagnostic potential of exhaled breath — at a price and form factor that makes real-world, home-based studies possible for the first time.

Every breath tells a story. We build the tools to read it.

Origin

Where We Started

Super Sensors grew out of years of hands-on research in breath VOC sensing for metabolic health applications. After developing and deploying a breath sensor platform in a real-world clinical study — collecting data from diabetic patients in their homes — the team recognized that the platform itself was the product.

No comparable tool existed at an accessible price point. Super Sensors was founded to bring that platform to the broader research and diagnostic community.

"We didn't set out to build a product company. We set out to solve a research problem. The product emerged when we realized nobody else had solved it."
